Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQs section! Yahan aapko un sabhi common sawalon ke jawab milenge jo humse aksar puchhe jaate hain. Agar aapko apne AC repair, service, ya installation se related koi confusion ho, toh yahaan zaroor dekhein.

1. Mera AC thanda hawa nahi de raha. Kya problem ho sakti hai?

Yeh issue AC filter clog hone, gas kam hone ya compressor problem ki wajah se ho sakta hai. Humare expert technicians detailed check karte hain aur best solution provide karte hain.

2. AC se paani tapak raha hai. Kya yeh normal hai?

Nahi, AC se paani tapakna ek technical fault hota hai. Iska reason blocked drain pipe ya ice jam ho sakta hai. Isse avoid karne ke liye timely service zaroori hai.

3. Aapka technician kitne time mein aata hai?

Aapki location aur booking time ke hisaab se hum same-day service provide karte hain. Emergency cases mein priority service bhi available hai.

4. Service charges kya hote hain?

Basic inspection ke baad hi exact charges bataye jaate hain. Hum transparent pricing follow karte hain—no hidden charges.

5. Kya aap log doorstep service dete hain?

Haan, hum 100% doorstep AC service provide karte hain. Aapko kahin jaane ki zarurat nahi—hum aapke ghar tak aate hain.

6. Kya aap gas refill bhi karte ho?

Ji haan. Hum R22, R32, aur R410a gases ki refill service offer karte hain, complete leak check ke saath.

7. Kya split aur window dono AC repair karte ho?

Bilkul! Chahe aapke paas window AC ho ya split AC, hum dono ki servicing, repairing, aur installation handle karte hain.

8. Warranty milti hai repair ke baad?

Haan, selected parts aur services par limited warranty milti hai. Technician aapko repair ke time proper details dega.

9. AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) available hai kya?

Haan, hum AMC plans provide karte hain jisme regular service, filter cleaning, aur emergency visit included hota hai—aur woh bhi pocket-friendly price par.

10. Booking kaise karein?

Booking karna simple hai! Aap hume direct call kar sakte hain, WhatsApp message bhej sakte hain, ya website par contact form fill karke request bhej sakte hain.
